# Term Of Service

Please see our Term Of Service before using TeamOne sheets add-on.

# What information do we collect?

We collect information from you when you contact us by email. This is the only time any data is collected or recorded.

# What do we use your information for?

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways

  • To improve our Add-on (we continually strive to improve our add-on offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you)
  • To improve customer service your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs)

# Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

Your information, whether public or private, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other company for any reason whatsoever.

# How do we protect your information?

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you access your personal information.

# Do we use cookies?

TeamOne add-on stores temporary data using cookies. For instance, we use cookies to help us remember and process the TeamOne add-on settings and options, for example, the last selected source and target TM1 environments or the last selected interface tab.

If you turn cookies off, some of the functionality or features may not function properly.

# Your Consent

By using TeamOne sheets add-on, you consent to our privacy policy.
